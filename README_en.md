## 📖 Overview

In May 2026, Amazon integrated Alexa Plus into its shopping experience, and Google announced the biggest redesign of search in 25 years. Neither announcement includes a design where humans scroll through product listings and choose.

The digital advertising market exceeds $740 billion. Google (77%), Meta (97%), Amazon (3rd revenue pillar) — the revenue structures of these companies are economically dependent on the act of "humans clicking."

The question of this book is clear. **What happens to the advertising model when AI agents buy on behalf of humans?**

In past advertising model transitions, the "surface" of attention changed — from print to web, from desktop to mobile. But the "subject" of attention was always human. In agentic commerce, **the subject of attention itself shifts from humans to AI**. It is not about where humans look. It is about humans no longer looking.

This is not a judgment. Not a comparison of superiority. This book is an open-source work whose purpose is to **describe a structural change currently underway, based on observable facts and data**.
